Analysis

Spring 2001, during an English course lecture analyzing a J.D. Salinger short story, our professor made an offhanded commented that the US Intelligence Agencies were in desperate need of analytic thinkers. When the 9/11 terrorist attack occured, I felt a sense of urgency, to make sense of the world, to take action and to find my true course in life. I left behind college in April 2003 for training in the US Air Force as a Network Intelligence Analyst.

I spent over 8 years proudly serving our country, living in Germany and South Korea, and finally settling in the central Maryland region. After leaving the service, I was fortunate to have the opportunity to become a contractor for the Department of Defense, working on the ever increasing Intelligence problems our Nation faces.

Software Dev

Another pivot and I am transitioning to a more technical field, working in the software development realm. Here, I found a renewed passion as a Systems Engineer in the role of a Product Owner (within the Agile software development framework). I've made some Balsalmiq mock-ups, utilized JIRA and other Atlassian products, made countless whiteboard drawings while chatting excitedly with my coworkers.

I'm interested in designing the user experience (UX), listening , understanding the underlying issues, implementing knowledge management practices, restructuring information architecture, working with folksonomies and taxonomies, helping write user stories to map software functionality.

I spent countless hours deconstructing and then reconstructing this process, reading and learning from as many experts in this field as possible. I've always enjoyed and gotten the most job satisfaction when I can help others in solving their problems. I enjoy learning and try my best to view problems as learning experiences.

Future Plans

My passion in life is story telling - reading, writing, listening closely to the tellers, creating an open, respectful dialog, seeing where there is room to improve a process or how to solve a problem. From that passion, I gravitated toward the user experience design process; I am also curious as to how to develop and implement streaming analytics.

Short term, within the next year, I will finish my B.S degree in Software Development from Western Governor's University.
